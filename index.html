How to Install

1. Go to Chrome Web Store and search for "GMaps Redirector".

2. Click on "Add to Chrome" button.

3. Pin it to the header of Google Chrome.

How to Use

1. Search for a location in Google Search.

2. Click on the GMaps Redirector extension.

3. Get redirected to the Google Maps page of your destination. Easy peasy!
