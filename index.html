How to Install

1. Go to Chrome Web Store and search for "GMaps in Search".

2. Click on "Add to Chrome" button.

3. Pin it to the header of Google Chrome.

How to Use

find it in the original place in the header

1. Search for a location in Google Search.

2. Under the search bar there should be "Pictures", "News" and other bookmarks. There you will find "Map", just click on it!

3. And you find yourself on the Google Maps with your destination. As it should be always.

jump there with one click to the Google Maps icon

1. Search for a location in Google Search.

2. Click on the extensions icon what is the Google Maps pin.

3. Get redirected to the Google Maps page of your destination. Easy peasy!
